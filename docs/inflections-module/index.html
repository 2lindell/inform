    • Article Inflection - To inflect "a" into "an", and so forth.

    • Name Clusters - Name clusters are sets of noun or adjective forms, perhaps multiple or in multiple languages, which have in common that they share a meaning.

    • Pluralisation - To form plurals of nouns.

    • Grading Adjectives - To inflect adjectives into comparative and superlative forms.

    • Past Participles - To inflect present into past participles.

    • Verb Conjugation - Conjugating verbs into the many different forms they can take.
