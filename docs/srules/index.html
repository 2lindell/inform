The Standard Rules extension, included in all Inform 7 works.

  • Preamble - The titling line and rubric, use options and a few other technicalities before the Standard Rules get properly started.

  • Physical World Model - Verbal descriptions of spatial relationships; the hierarchy of kinds of object, and their properties.

  • Variables and Rulebooks - The global variables and those built-in rulebooks which do not belong either to specific actions or to specific activities.

  • Activities - The built-in activities and their default stock of rules; the locale description mechanism.

  • Actions - The standard stock of actions supplied with Inform, along with the rules which define them; and the Understand grammar which corresponds to them.

  • Phrase Definitions - The phrases making up the Inform language, and in terms of which all other phrases and rules are defined; and the final sign-off of the Standard Rules extension, including its minimal documentation.
